I LOVE PHILIPPINES

The Philippines, officially the Republic of the Philippines, is an archipelago of about 7,641 island.

PHILLIPINES is a country in Southeast Asia

here are the best locations in the Philippines:

CEBU

Cebu, officially the Province of Cebu, is a province of the Philippines located in the Central Visayas region, and consists of a main island and 167 surrounding islands and islets

BORACAY

Boracay is a resort island in the Western Visayas region of the Philippines, located 0.8 kilometers off the northwest coast of Panay.

PALAWAN

Palawan is an archipelagic province of the Philippines that is located in the region of Mimaropa.

SIARGAO

Siargao is a tear-drop shaped island in the Phillippin Sea situated 196kilometers southeast of Tacloban

BALER

Baler offers a captivating blend of scenic beauty, surfing opportunities, and cultural heritage.

BOHOL

Bohol, a province in the Philippines, is celebrated for its unique attractions, including the Chocolate Hills, the tarsier sanctuary, and pristine beaches.
